# Sample PRD — Resume Kit

## Problem
Job seekers send the same resume to every application. Recruiters and AI screeners filter based on keyword matching. Most resumes don't speak the same language as the job description. Result: qualified people get filtered out before a human ever sees their application.

## User Story
As a job seeker, I want to paste a job description and get specific feedback on what's missing from my resume, so that I can tailor my application and get past the initial screen.

## Target Users
- Job seekers across all roles (engineer, designer, PM, analyst, marketer)
- 2-10 years experience
- Applying to 10+ jobs and not hearing back

## Solution
A tool that:
1. Accepts a job description (paste or URL)
2. Accepts a resume (upload or paste)
3. Compares the two and identifies gaps
4. Suggests specific changes to match the JD language
5. Works on any AI coding platform (Claude, Cursor, Gemini, etc.)

## Success Metrics
- Number of resume kit users (target: 200 in first month)
- Completion rate (user finishes the full flow)
- Return usage (user comes back for a second job application)

## Out of Scope
- Auto-applying to jobs
- Resume design/formatting
- Cover letter generation (maybe later)

- Landing page on theainativepm.com
