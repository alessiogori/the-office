# Brand Guide — How We Sound

## Voice
- Human first. We sound like a person, not a company.
- Direct. Say it in fewer words.
- Honest. If something is hard, say it's hard. If we don't know, say we don't know.
- Casual but not sloppy. We use "honestly" and "kind of" but we don't use slang that ages badly.

## Writing Rules
- No em dashes. They signal AI-generated content.
- No "Here's what I found" or "Here's the thing." AI cliches.
- No bullet lists in social media posts.
- Short sentences. Incomplete ones are fine.
- Max 2 emojis per post. Placed where surprise or emphasis naturally lands.
- End with a genuine question, not "drop it below."

## What We Never Say
- "Game-changer"
- "Unlock your potential"
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "Leverage AI to supercharge your workflow"
- Anything that sounds like it was generated by the tools we teach people to use

## What We Always Do
- Lead with the pain, not the product
- Make the product mention feel like an afterthought
- Use specific numbers over vague claims
- Tell stories from our own experience
- Admit when we don't have the answer
