# Thesis — What We Believe

## Core Belief
AI tools should make individuals as powerful as teams. One person with the right system can build, ship, and grow a product that used to require a full company.

## Who We Serve
People who want to build things but have been told they need a team, a budget, or permission first. Engineers who want to do product work. PMs who want to ship code. Marketers who want to build tools. Anyone who's tired of waiting.

## How We Win
1. Build in public. The process is the marketing.
2. Give everything away for free. Trust compounds faster than revenue.
3. Ship fast, learn faster. Perfection is the enemy of momentum.
4. Stay small. Complexity kills speed. Every new tool, process, or platform needs to earn its place.

## What We Won't Do
- Gate content behind paywalls or email captures
- Chase vanity metrics over real impact
- Build features nobody asked for
- Pretend AI replaces thinking. It replaces typing.

## The Test
Before building anything, ask: "Would I use this myself?" If the answer isn't an immediate yes, don't build it.
